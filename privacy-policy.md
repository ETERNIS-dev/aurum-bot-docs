Aurum Bot — Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 22, 2026
What data we collect:
Aurum collects and stores the following data necessary for bot functionality: Discord user IDs, server (guild) IDs, XP and level progress, wallet and vault balances, cooldown timestamps, purchased items and titles, and server configuration settings such as prefix, roles, and cooldowns.
How we use it:
All data is used exclusively to operate the bot's economy and leveling features. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any purpose outside of bot functionality.
Where it's stored:
Data is stored securely in a MongoDB database. Only the bot developer has access to this database.
Data retention:
Your data is retained as long as you or your server uses the bot. You may request deletion of your data by contacting the developer.
Third parties:
We do not share data with any third parties. The bot is hosted on Railway and uses MongoDB Atlas for storage — both have their own privacy policies.
Contact:
For data requests or questions, contact the developer through the bot's Discord support server.
